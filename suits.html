SUITS

A smart, stylish legal drama streaming on Netflix

Genre: Legal Drama

Created By: Aaron Korsh

Seasons: 9

Original Run: 2011 – 2019

Network: USA Network (Now streaming on Netflix)

About the Series

SUITS follows the story of Mike Ross, a brilliant college dropout with a photographic memory, who lands a job at one of New York’s top law firms despite never attending law school. He teams up with Harvey Specter, one of the best corporate lawyers in the city. Together, they win cases while hiding Mike’s biggest secret.

Main Characters

Harvey Specter

A confident, sharp, and fearless senior partner at Pearson Hardman. Known for his intelligence, style, and unbeatable courtroom presence.

Mike Ross

A genius with a photographic memory who practices law without a degree. His brilliance and ethics make him one of the firm’s strongest assets.
